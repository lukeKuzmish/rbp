Rules

This game requires at least two players or you're just an alcoholic!
A player guesses the next set of cards. "Black" represents two cards of black suits, "red" two cards of red suits, and "purple" a combination of the two. If you guess correctly, you get to hand out two drinks (one for each card). Both drinks can be given to one person, or you can split them up between players. If you guess wrong, you take one drink.
